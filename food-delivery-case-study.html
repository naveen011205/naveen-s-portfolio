Food Delivery App

Role: UI/UX Designer

Duration: 3 Weeks

Tools: Figma, FigJam

Platform: Mobile & Web

Problem Statement

Ordering food online should be quick and convenient, but many food delivery apps have cluttered interfaces, lengthy checkout processes, and poor navigation. Users often struggle to discover restaurants, compare menu items, track orders, and complete payments efficiently.

The objective was to design a modern, intuitive, and user-friendly food delivery application that simplifies the entire ordering journey—from discovering restaurants to tracking deliveries in real time.

Project Goal

Design a seamless food delivery experience that enables users to:

  • Browse nearby restaurants
  • Search and filter meals
  • Place orders with minimal steps
  • Track deliveries in real time
  • Save favorite restaurants and dishes
  • Complete secure online payments

Target Users

Primary Users

  • Students
  • Working professionals
  • Families

Secondary Users

  • Restaurant owners
  • Delivery partners

User Research

Research included analyzing existing food delivery platforms and gathering feedback from frequent online food ordering users.

Key Findings

  • Users wanted faster checkout
  • Restaurant filtering was often confusing
  • Menu pages felt overcrowded
  • Order tracking lacked clarity
  • Repeat ordering should require fewer steps

Design Decisions

  • Reduced checkout steps to improve speed
  • Used large food images for easier browsing
  • Simplified navigation with a bottom navigation bar
  • Maintained consistent spacing and reusable UI components

Challenges

  • Organizing large restaurant menus
  • Making search and filters intuitive
  • Designing a quick yet secure checkout experience

Outcome

The final design provides a clean and engaging food ordering experience with simplified navigation, faster checkout, and real-time order tracking. The interface helps users discover restaurants easily and complete orders with minimal effort.

Key Learnings

  • Simple navigation improves task completion
  • Visual hierarchy enhances food discovery
  • Streamlined checkout increases usability
  • Consistent design systems improve scalability
